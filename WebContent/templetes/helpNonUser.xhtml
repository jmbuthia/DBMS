1. Who should register and why?
Every person who is over 18 years, wishes to join motorbike sacco as rider, owner or general customer. Registration details is needed in order to login.

2. Why is date of birth needed when registering?
Date of birth together with the id number as well as cell phone number would be required when you forget your password and wishes to reset it.

3. How to ensure password safety?
Ensuring that you are the only person who knows it, it is not made up of common words and is more than 6 character,have atleast one uppercase, a number and easy to remember.

4. How can i become a rider or owner?
If you have attended a driving school and hold a renewed driving license with classes (F and G), you are legible to become a rider. If you own a motorbike and you have its legal documents (logbook), you can join and become a motorbike owner.

5. Is it possible to rider and own many motorbike?
Yes riders can own many motorbike but ride only one.

6. How are rider and owners Paid?
Motorbike riders have salary account and receive their money on a monthly bases. Each motorbike has an account and the owner is associated with this account, he/she can withdraw money from this account.
